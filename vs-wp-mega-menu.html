WP Mega Menu by Themeum is a solid, free drag-and-drop mega-menu builder. Giuliomax Menu Builder is also free — but it goes further, bundling 50 themes, a footer builder, an announcement bar, a WooCommerce cart icon and visibility rules into a single plugin. Here's an honest side-by-side.

Quick verdict

Both plugins are free and open-source, and both build proper mega menus. WP Mega Menu is laser-focused on a drag-and-drop submenu builder; its Pro add-on adds features such as a vertical menu and a feature-box widget. Giuliomax Menu Builder covers the mega menu and the rest of your navigation — themes, footer, announcement bar, WooCommerce, mobile styles and accessibility — without a paid tier. If you want an all-in-one navigation suite, Giuliomax does more for the same price: nothing.

What WP Mega Menu does well

  • A genuinely capable, free drag-and-drop builder for multi-row, multi-column submenus.
  • Lets you drop WordPress widgets, shortcodes and a search bar directly into menu panels.
  • Save menu themes and reuse them across menus.
  • Backed by Themeum, a well-known theme and plugin vendor.

Where Giuliomax wins

  • It's a full navigation suite, not just a mega menu — footer builder, announcement bar with countdown, and a WooCommerce cart icon are all included.
  • 50 ready-made themes in 10 categories, applied in one click — no styling from scratch.
  • Visibility rules by user role, device and schedule, free.
  • 4 mobile menu styles (dropdown, fullscreen, left/right drawer) plus sticky and scroll effects.
  • Accessibility baked in — WCAG 2.2 AA and full keyboard navigation.
  • Native Gutenberg block for Full Site Editing.

Which should you choose?

Choose WP Mega Menu if all you need is a free, flexible drag-and-drop mega-menu builder and you like dropping widgets into panels. Choose Giuliomax Menu Builder if you want the mega menu plus themes, a footer, an announcement bar, WooCommerce and accessibility in one free plugin. Both cost nothing — so it's worth trying Giuliomax to see how much it covers out of the box.
